STUDY OF THE DOMAIN

IDEA

"The true test of a king is not his birth, but his worth"
- Merlin, The Sword in the Stone

King Arthur Pendragon is the legendary British leader who, according to medieval histories and romances, led the defense of Britain against Saxon invaders in the late 5th and early 6th centuries.

The Sword in the Stone narrative symbolizes divine selection and the idea that true leadership comes from merit rather than birthright.
